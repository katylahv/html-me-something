❀ Frenchie ❀

Living History

Frenchie was born in Jefferson City, MO, grew up in Columbia, MO, and lived in Boston, MA for two years. Currently, French resides in Saint Louis, MO.

Early Years

As an orphaned puppy destined for euthanization, the little angel otherwise known as Frenchie was adopted in Columbia, Missouri on September 11th 2011. As a puppy, Frenchie loved to bother the older dogs she lived with, eat her mom's socks, and climb to the top of staircases only to whine until mom came to rescue her because she couldn't quite figure out how to get back down.

Breed

Although Frenchie's exact breed is unknown< because her owner is too cheap to get a DNA test, it is agreed upon by at least two vets that she's probably just a deer-head Chihuahua.
